# Data Analytics Capstone Projects

Complete portfolio of 3 end-to-end data analytics projects analyzing 541K+ e-commerce transactions.

## 📊 Projects Overview

### 1️⃣ SQL E-Commerce Analytics
Advanced SQL analysis of 541K+ transaction records with business intelligence insights
- **15 SQL queries** covering RFM analysis, customer lifetime value, churn detection
- **Key insight:** Top 26% of customers generate 80% of revenue
- **Repeat customer rate:** 99.50% (strong customer loyalty)
- 📁 Folder: `01-SQL-Analysis/`

### 2️⃣ ETL Pipeline (SSIS)
Production-ready data pipeline extracting, transforming, and loading e-commerce data
- Extract 541K+ records from OLE DB source
- Transform with data validation, type conversions, calculated columns
- Load to SQL Server destination with error handling
- 📁 Folder: `02-ETL-Pipeline/`

### 3️⃣ Power BI Dashboard
Interactive 3-page business intelligence dashboard with synchronized filtering
- 8+ KPI cards displaying revenue, orders, customers
- 2 data tables (Top 20 Customers, Top 20 Products)
- Multiple visualizations: pie chart, line chart, stacked bar chart, gauge chart
- Synchronized slicers for dynamic filtering by date range and customer segment
- 📁 Folder: `03-PowerBI-Dashboard/`

## 🎯 Business Insights Discovered

### Customer Segmentation
- **VIP Customers:** Recency ≤30 days, Frequency ≥5, Monetary ≥$1,000
- **Gold Customers:** Recency ≤90 days, Frequency ≥3, Monetary ≥$500
- **Regular Customers:** Stable repeat purchasers
- **At-Risk Customers:** No purchase in 180+ days

### Revenue Analysis
- Top 26% customers = 80% of total revenue (Pareto principle)
- Revenue concentrated in low-value orders (<$50)
- Top product (DOTCOM POSTAGE) generated $3.96M revenue
- 38 countries represented with UK as largest market

### Customer Behavior
- 99.50% customers are repeat purchasers (high loyalty)
- Average order value: $18.30
- Average customer lifetime value across top 30: $30K-$87K+
- Seasonal patterns visible in monthly trends

### Product Performance
- Top 15 products account for significant portion of revenue
- Wide price range across product catalog
- Some products are niche items (1 customer)
- Opportunity for bundling and cross-selling
